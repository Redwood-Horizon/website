What We Do

Whole-Person Care Coordination

We align housing, health, behavioral health, and social services into a single, coherent support plan designed around each individual.

Learn More →

Community-Based Wraparound

Our team meets you where you are — in your home, in your community — providing consistent, long-term support that adapts as your needs change.

Learn More →

Sustainable, Long-Term Engagement

Stability takes time. We stay alongside our clients for as long as they need us, navigating transitions and adjusting supports through every stage.

Who We're For

Redwood Horizon serves adults and families with complex, intersecting needs — including those navigating housing instability, chronic health conditions, behavioral health challenges, re-entry from incarceration, and systemic barriers to care. If you or someone you care about needs coordinated, whole-person support, we're here.
