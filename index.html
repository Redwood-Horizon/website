What We Do

We provide whole-person, community-based wraparound services that help people facing barriers to stability stay connected to care and community.

Whole-Person Support

Care coordination across health, behavioral health, housing, benefits, employment, and re-entry. We start with what matters most to you — not what fits a checkbox.

Community-Based Care

Services happen where you live and where you need them. We come to you — in your home, your community, or wherever is safest and most comfortable.

Persistent Engagement

We stay with people through complexity, on their timeline. We don't disappear when things get hard. When one door closes, we find another way.

Who We're For

Redwood Horizon is for people with complex needs who are navigating more than one challenge at once — housing instability, a behavioral health condition, a return from incarceration, or difficulty accessing care and benefits. If the system hasn't worked for you, we're here to help figure out a different path. We serve adults and families across Northern California who need someone in their corner, no matter how many systems they're navigating.
